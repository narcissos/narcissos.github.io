Digital authoring taught me about authenticity. I’m not saying spill your life story out for total strangers… but who cares about telling universal stories that everyone can relate to? What about your own story? Whether it’s your gender, ethnicity, or anything else… there’s value in your experience, shared or not.

Lately, I’ve been interested in visual novels—text-based video games featuring art, music, and interactivity. Readers have stakes as they alter the story with their choices. Open-sourced programs like Twine and game engines like Unity have given me these affordances. Of course, learning the required languages of JavaScript and Python is easier said than done, but with a little (or a lot of) hard work… it’s within reach.

And I’m sure, either in a few weeks or months or years, I’ll find something else that interests me, and keep learning and growing as a digital author.
